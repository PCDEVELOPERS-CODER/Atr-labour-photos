LOGIN PAGE:

  • First, log in using your username and password by clicking the "ENTER" button. Make sure to turn on your location before logging into the app.

GALLERY PAGE:

  • On the gallery screen, tap the top "ADD" button to enter your details.
  • On the gallery page, you will see all the details you have submitted.

DATAENTRY PAGE:

  • On the Data Entry page, there are multiple fields. The submit button will work only if at least four columns are filled.
  • Then, there is a "History" button to view your entered details.

CHANGE PASSWORD PAGE:

  • On the Change Password page, enter your registered name, current password, and new password. Confirm the new password, then click the "Update Password" button.
  • Your password will be updated successfully.
